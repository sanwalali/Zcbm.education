English Language Competency:

We offer English as a Foreign Language (EFL) classes to all of our learners to improve their English Language ability and Academic writing skills. It helps learners to communicate well in English. Also our medium of Curriculum delivery is in English Language.



Numeracy or Mathematics Ability:

We are committed to assist students to improve their numeracy ability. Lecturers are assisting students regularly to facilitate access to mathematics, understand the importance of mathematics, enjoy its role and impact in the real world.



Information and Communications Technology (ICT):

We live in a digital world in which Information Communication Technology (ICT) continues to play most vital role. It helps to meet increasing demands of our workplace and our daily life. Regardless of learners’ chosen qualifications (e.g. Accounting Finance or Business Studies or Health and Social Care or Travel and Tourism) to study with us, we will offer all learners to attend one of our ICT modules to improve their ICT skills.



Discussion on Past Assessments:

We regularly arrange discussion, mock assessments and workshops in relation to the topics taught and the past exam papers (assessment). This approach in classroom activities assists to understand the assessment methods, curriculum and tracking the improvement required.
