Welcome to ZCBM


Welcome to Zanzibar College of Business & Management (ZCBM) at Zanzibar, Tanzania in East Africa. Our experienced Academics are highly qualified in their field of teaching. We prepare, develop and encourage learners to be ambitious and focused to achieve their future goals.

Our curriculum is focused to raise the standards of teaching and learning in Zanzibar, Tanzania, East Africa. ZCBM curriculum emphasises the importance of improving the learners’ knowledge, develop skills in literacy, numeracy, science, and information technology.

At the college, the learners are supported to be able to overcome fears and barriers. Our offered innovative curriculum prepares young students for today’s job and make them ready to be able to fight against tomorrow’s unemployment through the life-long learning.

Promoting Equality and Diversity at the College: We welcome students from all backgrounds in East Africa, we maintain the diversity inclusion, we provide with equal opportunity to our students and staff. ZCBM promotes creativity, flexibility and inclusion of co-operative learning methods. We consider every students must be able to function effectively and efficiently within this ever changing global society. The college is committed to encourage all students to develop problem solving nature or willingly participate the coherent team by reflective thinking.

SCHOLARSHIP

ZCBM offers scholarship up to 20% waived tuition fees to the meritorious students with excellent education background and also if the students are recommended by the Tanzanian Government.

WELFARE

Advice is given to all of our students about all kinds of problems from losing any documents, to finding a local doctor. We offer help to open a bank account. We will try our best to make sure that our students’ time with us enjoyable and great learning experience. Every assistance is given to all students to ensure that they should enrol on a course that is appropriate, and of real benefit to them. Students will be encouraged to consult members of staff about their progress and to discuss any topics they find difficulties.

BOOKS - LIBRARY – INTERNET

Each class will follow a course handbook according to the ZCBM and ICM modules’ guideline, and will be supplemented by our extensive resources of other materials (e.g. article, journal etc.). Normally, all the recommended text books and accompanying work books are available in the ZCBM Library. We advise students to have their course books with them when they attend classes. Students and teachers can borrow books from the ZCBM library. The free Internet room is available every working day from 10-00am to 05-30pm. Students wishing to relax can go to the Coffee room.

The Library is publicly available on a FREE membership basis, (terms & condition applies).

SOCIAL PROGRAMMES

The college arranges wide range of afternoon, weekend and evening activities (including sightseeing tours and visiting places of historical and cultural interest to entertain and provide overseas students with insights about social culture of Zanzibar). We organise social events for our students during the year, which include: parties, evenings in cafes, restaurants, visit to museums, parks etc.
