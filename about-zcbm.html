About ZCBM

Zanzibar College of Business & Management was incorporated in April 2011 to operate its activities to establish its educational projects in Zanzibar, Tanzania. Since then, We are proud to announce our 10 years of journey in Zanzibar. ZCBM has developed its Excellence and proud to offer its Educational Services to the community over the last 10 years.

We offer bespoke education and training programmes which are fit for the purpose to develop learners, practitioners and professionals to take their next challenges.

We are ICM (Institute of Commercial Management www.icm.education) approved training and examination centre in Tanzania. We offer wide range of Professional qualifications progressing to Higher Education in the UK Universities.
